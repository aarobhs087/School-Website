About us

The institution operates under the motto "Service before Self," focusing on imparting value-oriented, quality education with an emphasis on national integration. DPS Smart School highlights the collaborative efforts of management, teachers, students, parents, and the community in continually improving their educational quality.

DPS Smart School, strives to create an environment that fosters personal growth and academic excellence through a blend of academics, activities, and sports. It aims to produce responsible, knowledgeable, and culturally aware individuals prepared for a global society.
